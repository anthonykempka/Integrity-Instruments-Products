USBM400 Special considerations.

If you have a USB module, before opening the utility, connect the USB module and power it. The “USB hardware detected” icon will be implemented. Now you can open the utility.

When you want to disconnect the USB module, click the icon to “safely remove hardware”. You can then remove the module.

When you configure the module, you must disconnect the module as previously described, close the utility, and repeat the process of connecting the module and opening the utility.
